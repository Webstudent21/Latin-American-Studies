The Haitian revolution is a major slave revolt that happened in Saint domingue between 1791-1804.The Haitian revolution was inspired by the french revolution and was the results of years of built up tension between the different social classes and there were many smaller slave revolts that happened prior to the Haitian revolution.Saint domingue was a wealthy colony primarily because of the high production of goods like coffee,sugar and cotton.However this came at the expense of the slaves who were forced to work without any compensation.This was not sustainable long term as the slaves grew tired of being treated poorly and wanted freedom and independence from the plantation owners who were opressing them.The french revolution was similar because peasants were also an opressed group in france and were heavily taxed and they were tired of living in a monarchy were the king decided what was best for everybody so they overthrew the monarchy and established a republic instead.During The Haitian Revolution slaves and free colored people fought to overthrow the white planters who ruled over them for so long and finally gained indepence from both slave owners and the french.
