Important People and events

Social Classes

Planters- Wealthy Plantation owners who owned many slaves as well

Petit blancs-Ordinary citizens with normal occupations(Teachers,Shop keepers etc.)

Free blacks-Colored People who were free and usually more wealthy than petit blancs

Slaves-Enslaved Colored People who had the least amount of rights and wealth

Maroons-Runaway slaves who sought refuge in maroon communities.

Declaration of the rights of man and citzens

In 1789 the Declaration of the rights of man and citizen was created.This document stated that all men were created equal and that all men had certain natural inalienable rights.This Document is significant because it helped spark the Haitian Revolution.While the Declaration claimed that all men are equal and should be granted rights in reality both free and enslaved people did not have the same rights as Plantation owners.This blatant double standard encourage people of color to revolt which is part of the reason why the Haitian revolution began.The declaration of the rights of man claimed that all men are created but this document did not apply to people of color and women.Which is why Marie gouze wrote the declaration of women's rights to show that women were excluded from what was stated in the declaration.This civil rights document essentially added fuel to the fire by excluding the most oppressed groups like people of color and women.

Toussaint L'Ouveture

Toussaint L'Ouverture was a former slave who was born into slavery on May 20,1743. Toussaint was the son of an African Prince who was captured and enslaved.Even though Toussaint was a slave growing up he had a good education his godfather simon baptiste taught him how to read and write.He was also granted access to his own personal library by the owner of the plantation he was born on.As a result he was very well educated by the age of 20.He was capable of speaking three languages Creole,French and Latin and he also had a lot of knowledge when it comes to medicinal herbs and plants.He became a free man in 1776 which is when he decided to get married and have two sons.Even Though Toussaint was already free he was inspired to help free enslaved Haitians after hearing about a slave revolt that happened in august 1791.Toussaint eventually created his own army and taught them guerilla warfare tactics.Toussaint would eventually gained control over san domingo and give himself the title general in chief of army.However Napoleon bonaparte did not like this and he wanted san domingo to be under french control again.So he sent his brother to along with an Army to capture L'Ouverture.
