What caused the Haitian Revolution?

There was a lot of social tension and many slave revolts that happened before the Haitian Revolution so you may be asking yourself what exactly caused something as significant as the Haitian Revolution? Well here are some factors that helped create the perfect circumstances for the Haitian Revolution to take place.

The French Revolution- The Haitian revolution was inspired by the French Revolution Which took place between 1787 and 1799. The French Revolution has some similarities to the Haitian Revolution because in The French Revolution Peasants were revolting against the monarchy due to them being heavily taxed and having poor living conditions compared to the rich. The Haitian Revolution is similar because an oppressed group of people are fighting for their freedom and independence.The enslaved colored people of Saint domingue where inspired by the French Revolution and were ready to fight for their rights as well.
